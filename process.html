My 5 Step Process

Working with me means diving into a creative journey where your vision is at the forefront.

1. DISCOVERY

This is the first step in preparing the groundwork for our creative journey! Here, we hop on a call to explore, comprehend, and synchronize our visions for your project.

My primary objective is to grasp your goals, target demographic, and the purpose of your design project. I’ll ask insightful questions to discover specific aspects of your vision, desired results, and what you aim to achieve with this project. Just a regular conversation to get to know each other.

Following this, a Project Proposal detailing the terms of the design work will be dispatched, accompanied by a 50% Deposit Invoice to be settled before we proceed to Step 2.

2. RESEARCH

Following our Discovery Call, I’ll start doing some research. This involves examining your competitors, exploring various design styles, and generally assessing the potential direction for this project. Armed with this information, I’ll craft two moodboards (or sometimes just one if we’re already decided on the style) and I’ll send these over to you to choose the direction of the brand.

Once you’re pleased with the direction we’re headed, we can proceed to step 3, where the magic truly unfolds.

3. CREATION

I kick off by immersing myself in the project, leveraging the insights taken from our discovery call. I brainstorm concepts, sketch out ideas, and dive into various design avenues that resonate with your goals and preferences.

The sketching stage is where I bring ideas to life, dedicating ample time to refining and polishing. It’s during this phase that I filter out any generic clutter.

I experiment with color schemes, typography, imagery, and layout compositions, continuously iterating to ensure the design resonates with your brand identity and goals.

4. REFINING

This is where we nail the project. After I’ve sent the core designs to you for review and feedback, I focus on fine-tuning every detail. Refinement involves polishing the designs and making necessary adjustments. This is where I can apply the same style to the rest of the brands assets to maintain consistency and really develop your strong brand identity.

Not only am I meeting your expectations, I aim to exceed them.

5. FINALIZE

The culmination of the creation stage involves presenting the final designs to you. I’ll prepare a comprehensive brand presentation showcasing the evolution of the designs and highlighting the thought process behind each decision. This presentation serves as an opportunity to discuss the design journey, address any final feedback, and ensures your satisfaction before delivering the finalized design assets.

Now your brand is ready to take over the world!
